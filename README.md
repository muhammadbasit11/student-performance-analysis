📊 Student Performance Analysis

Project Overview:
This project explores a dataset of student academic performance to uncover insights about GPA, study habits, and extracurricular activities. The goal was to analyze patterns and correlations that influence student success.

Key Features & Analysis:

-Calculated GPA statistics: mean, range, and student rankings
-Analyzed GPA by gender
-Studied impact of study time and absences on GPA
-Explored influence of activities: sports, music, volunteering
-Examined parental education and support effects on performance
-Ranked top and bottom performing students

Skills & Concepts Applied:

-Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
-Grouping & Aggregation (groupby, mean, sum)
-Feature Engineering (categorizing study time, activities)
-Correlation Analysis
-Ranking & Sorting

Key Takeaways:

-Learned how to extract actionable insights from educational datasets
-Strengthened understanding of Python, Pandas, and structured analysis
-Practiced combining multiple features for deeper insights
